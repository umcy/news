UPCOMING EVENTS
Sun, November 19 2017
Thanksgiving Dinner

Join with your church family in a Thankgiving Meal on Sunday, November 19, right after worship (starting at about 10:45 am). There will also be African Team Ministries items for sale. African Team Ministries helps artists from several different African countries make a living by selling their jewelry, crafts, musical instruments, and more. Money will go directly back to the crafters to support our brothers and sisters that live in very difficult circumstances.


Sun, December 10 2017
Christmas Concert

Rev. Phillips aspired to be an opera singer. For over 20 years he has held annual concerts in raising funds for international missions.

Jose Prieto and friend will perform guitar duos. Jose graduated from the Musicians Institute of Los Angeles and Mastered in Orchestration for films from the prestigious Berklee School of Music.







PAST EVENTS
Sun, November 5 2017
Come over to Fellowship Hall right after Worship (10:45am) for an authentic SSP meal and a narrated slideshow of our week of service and adventures to Smith River, CA.

Your lunch donations will kick off our fundraising for next year's trip to Tsaile, Arizona July 21 - 29, 2018. Youth that are going into 7th grade through finishing their senior year of High School in 2018 are eligible to come, and can hold their spot with a $50 payment. We will fundraise all of the remaining fees and expenses so that everyone can participate.

Sierra Service Project is a wonderful way for youth to experience a week of service and fun while developing a heart for Christ.


Sun, October 29 2017
The House of Faith proudly presents an afternoon of exquisite chamber and solo music provided by the highly-gifted musicians from the Idyllwild Arts Academy. Come and enjoy superb violin, cello, and piano music in the beauty of the stone and stained glass Sanctuary.
